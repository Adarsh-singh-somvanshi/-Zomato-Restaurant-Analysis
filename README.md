# 📌 Project Overview

The food delivery industry generates a massive amount of restaurant data every day. This project analyzes the **Zomato Bangalore Restaurant Dataset** to identify customer preferences, restaurant performance, pricing strategies, and geographical trends.

The analysis focuses on transforming raw restaurant data into meaningful business insights that can support better decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Restaurant owners and food delivery platforms need to understand:

- Which restaurant types are most popular?
- Which locations have the highest restaurant density?
- Does online ordering improve customer ratings?
- Does table booking influence customer satisfaction?
- Is there any relationship between pricing and ratings?
- Which cuisines dominate the market?

Answering these questions helps businesses optimize operations, improve customer experience, and maximize profitability.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Perform comprehensive data cleaning.
- Handle missing values and duplicates.
- Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA).
- Visualize restaurant trends.
- Generate actionable business insights.
- Provide recommendations for business growth.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

### 🍽️ Restaurant Types

- Casual Dining dominates the Bangalore restaurant market.
- Quick Bites and Cafés are the second and third most common restaurant categories.

---

### ⭐ Customer Ratings

- Most restaurants receive ratings between **3.5 and 4.2**.
- Very few restaurants have ratings below **2.5**.

---

### 🛵 Online Ordering

- Restaurants offering online ordering generally receive slightly better customer ratings.

---

### 💰 Cost Analysis

- Higher-priced restaurants do not necessarily receive better ratings.
- Customer experience appears to influence ratings more than pricing.

---

### 📍 Popular Locations

- Certain locations contain significantly more restaurants than others, indicating higher customer demand.

---

### 🔥 Correlation

- Restaurant ratings have only a weak relationship with cost.
- Service quality likely plays a greater role in customer satisfaction.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand operations in high-demand locations.
- Improve online ordering experience.
- Focus on customer service rather than increasing prices.
- Promote high-performing cuisines.
- Encourage table booking for premium restaurants.
